FAQ

Short answers to reduce customer hesitation.

How do I use the locker system?

Place your laundry bag in a locker, note the locker number, then submit the order form with that locker number.

How fast is turnaround?

Most orders are completed quickly. If you ever need a rush, include it in “Special requests” and we’ll confirm availability.

What does “$25 minimum” mean?

Orders have a $25 minimum charge. This keeps the service efficient and helps maintain consistent quality and turnaround.

Can you handle sensitive skin / fragrance-free?

Yes — write it in “Special requests.” You can request fragrance-free, specific detergent, or no softener.
