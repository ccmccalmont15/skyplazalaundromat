FAQ

Short answers to remove hesitation.

How do I use the locker system?

Put your laundry in a locker, remember the locker number, then submit the form with that same locker number.

How fast is turnaround?

Most orders are completed quickly. If you need a rush, put it in “Special requests” and we’ll confirm availability.

What does “$25 minimum” mean?

Orders have a $25 minimum charge to keep the service efficient and maintain consistent quality and turnaround.

Can you do fragrance-free / sensitive skin?

Yes. Put it in “Special requests.” You can request fragrance-free, specific detergent, or no softener.
